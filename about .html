Our Story & Team

The Green Thumbs Difference

Green Thumbs Nursery was founded in 2018 by two passionate botanists who wanted to create a community hub for plant lovers. Our mission is to promote sustainable gardening practices and enhance the natural beauty of our local environment.

Our Core Values

  • **Quality:** We only stock the healthiest, most vigorous plants.
  • **Sustainability:** We use and promote eco-friendly growing methods.
  • **Education:** We empower our customers with expert knowledge.
